Who We Are

EYD (Embrace Your Dreams) is an independent studio driven by technical excellence and narrative passion. We combine engineering precision with artistic vision to deliver high-quality entertainment and support other studios through specialized outsourcing and tool development.

Our Expertise

Strategic solutions for Studios and Businesses.

Game Development & Co-Dev

We don't just build games; we help build yours. From full-cycle indie development to specialized modules for AAA productions, we offer robust engineering solutions in Unity, Godot, and Unreal.

3D Modeling & Pixel Art

From Concept to Final Asset. Our art department delivers high-fidelity 3D models (Low/High Poly), distinct 2D Pixel Art, and UI design, tailored to fit any aesthetic requirement.

Engine Tools & Automation

Accelerate your production. We create custom editor tools, project structures, and automation scripts to streamline workflows and reduce technical debt in major game engines.

Immersive & Metaverse

Next-gen Digital Assets. We design immersive metaverse experiences and blockchain-integrated assets (NFTs, Tokens) ready for marketplaces like OpenSea, creating real value within virtual economies.
